The Enigma of Cal Stone

Cal Stone, portrayed by Jack Messina for the majority of the series, is the youngest son of Ben and Grace Stone. His character is central to the show’s plot, not only because of his presence on the fateful flight but also due to his unique connection to the series’ supernatural elements, known as the ‘Callings’.

At the outset of the series, Cal’s diagnosis with leukemia serves as a catalyst for the Stone family’s participation in Flight 828. His initial disappearance and subsequent return with the rest of the passengers sets the stage for the family’s tumultuous journey.

Cal’s Connection to the ‘Callings’

Cal’s experiences with the ‘Callings’—mysterious voices and visions that guide the passengers—are more intense and frequent than those of other characters. This connection positions him as a kind of ‘chosen one’ within the narrative.

Cal’s Age Jump and Transformation

One of the most shocking twists in Manifest is Cal’s sudden age jump. In the third season finale, Cal touches the tailfin of the plane and disappears, only to return as a teenager, now portrayed by actor Ty Doran. The age jump is not only a physical transformation but also signifies a deeper connection to the show’s mysteries. Cal’s new age aligns with the time he would have been had Flight 828 not disappeared, suggesting a correction or realignment of time.
