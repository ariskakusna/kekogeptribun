Who Is Nicole Poturalski, Brad Pitt's New Girlfriend?

August 24, 2024
post

Amidst all the craziness of the ongoing divorce battle between Angelina Jolie and Brad Pitt, we have some new gossip that might shed some interesting light on the whole proceeding: Pitt, who along with Jolie was declared legally single, is dating someone new. And no, it's not Jennifer Aniston, although some fans will hold out for that relationship until the end of time. It's also not other women rumored to be in Pitt's life over the past couple of years, including Sienna Miller, Charlize Theron, and Alia Shawkat (Pitt is extremely private and hadn't confirmed anything about anyone he was seeing, although a source refuted the Theron rumors). Nope, Pitt's new love is 27-year-old model Nicole Poturalski, who hails from Poland and is a model splitting her time between California and Germany.

We just got an official sighting of the two, and outlets are confirming that the two are together. And Twitter has thoughts, but we'll get to that in a sec. What do we know about Poturalski so far?

Pitt and Poturalski may have been dating for nine months.

According to Us Weekly, the pair were just spotted getting off a plane and headed to Pitt's chateau property in the south of France (photos at that link, but they're very fuzzy). But Daily Mail found photos from the Hollywood Bowl way back in 2019, where the two are clearly flirting with each other—so, if that's the start of their relationship, they've been dating longer than any of us imagined.

Poturalski is a model.

Recruited at 13 at Disneyland Paris, Poturalski's been successful in Germany, making it into the German Harper’s Bazaar and Grazia. And she just made the cover of ELLE Germany:

They're reportedly "falling" for each other.

Us Weekly has the scoop:

“It’s currently a go-with-the-flow situation, and Nicole’s not running around yelling about her love for Brad from the rooftops...Nicole is in [LA] a lot for work. That’s where the bulk of her dates with Brad have been...Going by what Brad’s been saying, they are totally falling for each other."

But it's still super casual.

Even though they're reportedly "falling" for each other, the relationship is still staying very casual.

“Brad thinks Nicole is beautiful and kind,” a source told Us Weekly. “He has absolutely no plans nor any desire to ever get married again. Brad doesn’t want a girlfriend that he’s with every day.”

They're both dedicated parents.

Their casual attitude about the romance might have something to do with the fact that they're both parents and believe that their kids have to take top priority.

“They’re dedicated parents who fit in dating and other social engagements around their kids’ schedules,” the Us Weekly source explained.
