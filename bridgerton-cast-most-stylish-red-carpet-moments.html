    The third season, sees the blossoming relationship between Penelope Featherington (Nicola Coughlan) and Colin Bridgerton, (played by Luke Newton).

    When the cast is not swanning about "the ton" in their finery, luckily for us here at the Hello! Fashion offices, they are willing to spend yet more hours in the makeup chair getting dolled up and working with the industry's leading designers, in order to curate their looks for some of the UK's most prestigious events.

    Hello! Fashion takes a look at the Bridgerton cast's most stylish red carpet moments:

    1/18

    Florence Hunt at the S3 Pt II premiere

    Florence, who plays the role of Hyacinth, oozed modern 60s glamour in a deep blue polkadot Miu Miu set paired with Bulgari jewellery.

    2/18

    Luke Newton at the S3 Pt II premiere, London

    Mr Colin Bridgerton looked dapped in a grey suit with embellished shoulders by Emporio Armani. He brought the glamour with jewellery from Chopard.

    3/18

    Victor Alli at the S3 Pt II premiere, London

    Victor, who plays Francesca Bridgerton's love interest Lord John Stirling wore a crisp white suit by Alexander McQueen.

    4/18

    Jessica Madsen at the S3 Pt II premiere

    Jessica took mermaidcore to the red carpet in a Fendi couture bralette and midi skirt set.

    5/18

    Nicola Coughlan at the London S3 Pt II premiere, 2024

    Nicola gave regencycore a cool-girl makeover in a soft pink bardot gown from Rodarte. A pair of decadent red gloves and a headpiece with a veil created the most mesmerising look.

    6/18

    Simone Ashley, S3 Pt II premiere, London

    In her typically cool It-girl fashion, Simone stunned in a Del Core piece from its FW24 RTW collection featuring a satin bralette and maxi skirt. She paired it with Suzanne Kalan jewellery.

    7/18

    Golda Rosheuvel at the S3 Pt II, 2024

    Queen Charlotte looked every inch the royal in a radiant blue cape gown by Mary Katrantzou. Can we take a moment for the statement gold headpiece in the front of her hair?

    8/18

    Phoebe Dynevor at the 2024 Met Gala

    Phoebe made fashion history by wearing the first-ever Victoria Beckham gown on the Met Gala red carpet.

    9/18

    Jonathan Bailey at the 2024 Met Gala

    Jonathan looked seriously suave in a monochromatic 'fit by Loewe.

    10/18

    Claudia Jessie at the Bridgerton Season 3 screening, 2024

    Claudia oozed chic in a black waistcoat and trousers ensemble paired with two-tone boots.

    11/18

    Adjoa Andoh at the 2023 NAACP Image Awards

    Adjoa Andoh stunned in a satin black pleated dress with 3D florals across the body.

    12/18

    Luke Newton at the 2023 Fashion Awards

    Luke AKA Colin Bridgerton perfected tonal dressing at the 2023 Fashion Awards, using a sequinned khaki shirt for a pop of colour.

    13/18

    Nicola Coughlan at the 2022 Met Gala

    At fashion's biggest night of the year Nicola Coughlan turned to London-based designer Richard Quinn for an unforgettable satin black and pink frou-frou puff-sleeve gown.

    14/18

    Simone Ashley at the 2021 Fashion Awards

    Simone Ashley opted for a black asymmetric co-ord by AZ Factory with a super dramatic leg slit and flowing train for the 2021 Fashion Awards.

    16/18

    Butterflies were on the agenda for Polly Walker who looked radiant in a pleated white midi dress featuring a dark grey print, courtesy of Mithridate.
