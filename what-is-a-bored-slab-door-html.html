A slab door is a plain, stripped-down door that does not feature a frame or any other decorative elements. It is the installer’s job to connect the slab door to an existing door frame or to build a completely new door frame for the slab door to be installed. Second, do slab doors come with holes pre-drilled into them?

Slab doors are delivered with no holes for the door knobs, and the hinges must be mortised out before they can be used.

And what is the significance of the term “pre bored door?”

It simply means that the door has already been attached to the frame, saving you the time and effort of mortising the frame and door in order for the hinges to be received.
