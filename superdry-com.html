FAQs

Does Superdry do free shipping?

No. Superdry does not have free shipping. Standard US shipping costs $7.95 for each order. This will usually take 4-7 working days to be delivered. You can always check this Marie Claire page for the occasional free shipping code. 

Does Superdry do student discount?

No. Superdry does not currently offer US students any discount. However, you can still save on your order by shopping in the sale section of their website. You can also use one of our promo codes to get 10-60% off your order.  

Does Superdry have a first order discount?

Yes. If you are new to Superdry, make sure to sign up for their email newsletter. Simply click our code or visit their website and scroll to the website footer. There will be a sign-up box where you can type your preferred email address. Once you have confirmed your email, you’ll get a 10% discount on your first order.  

When are the Superdry USA sales?

Superdry has sales all year with their ‘Sale’ page. Simply visit their website and click ‘Sale’ from the menu. Whether you’re shopping for a new Superdry jacket, coat, or t-shirt, you can pay less than RRP with the sale.  

Does Superdry have Black Friday sales?

Yes, Superdry usually has Black Friday sales. Last year, we saw discounts of up to 50% off selected clothes and 20% off every product. Make sure you sign up for Superdry emails or bookmark this page so you stay updated on the Black Friday sales.  

What is the return policy at Superdry?

You can return your Superdry purchase within 28 days of delivery. Returns can only be made online by the Superdry Returns Portal. You will need your order number, zip code, and email address. You can return your order for free to a Superdry store, otherwise, postal returns have a shipping cost of $6.95.
