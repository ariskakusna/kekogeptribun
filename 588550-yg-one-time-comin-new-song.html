One Time Comin'

August 24, 2024
post

YG warned us the other day that he had new music on the way, and here it is. Tonight, the West Coast spitter decides to release a powerful new single on iTunes/Apple Music called “One Time Comin'.”

Similar to the controversial “FDT (Fuck Donald Trump)” record, YG decides to once again be a voice of reason for the people & address the social injustices happening in America right now, including police brutality & racial discrimination. As the artwork depicts, the song is “in loving memory of the victims of police brutality.”

Hit play and let us know what you think.
