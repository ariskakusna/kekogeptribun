Is there an ALDO free shipping option?

Yes. If you're an ALDO Crew member, you can receive free shipping when you spend $50 or more when you are on the first and second tiers. When you get to the Crew VIP tier, you can receive free standard shipping when you spend just $10 or more.

How do I get a 15% off ALDO promo code?

You'll get a 15% ALDO welcome offer when you join the ALDO Crew loyalty program. You can join the program by clicking the 'Join Now' button found at the bottom of the homepage. 

Does ALDO have student discount?

ALDO doesn't always offer a student discount code however, at the time of writing, students and graduates can receive an ALDO discount code of 15%. Simply verify your student status with Student Beans and your ALDO code will arrive with you straight away. 

Does ALDO have price adjustments?

Yes. ALDO has price adjustments for items you have bought within 7 days of purchase. Online orders cannot be adjusted to in-store prices. You also cannot get price adjustments for Black Friday, Cyber Monday, First-Order discounts, and other savings. 

What is the ALDO return policy?

ALDO states their return policy is 60 days. Your items should be returned in their original condition with the original receipt. Final sale items cannot be returned. There are some exclusions to the returns policy so make sure to check the ALDO website.
