The Millennial Mountains quest offerings location in Genshin Impact

August 08, 2024
post

One of the new Genshin Impact world quests in The Chasm sends you around the upper area to find six different offerings for Wang. Our Genshin Impact Millennial Mountains quest walkthrough explains where to find each of the six offerings.

Flower of Farsight location

The Flower of Farsight is on the northern peak, near an NPC. The NPC is standing by three flowers. Picking them will give you the flower, as well as a new side quest to complete.

Skyfeather location

The Skyfeather is in a bird’s nest at the location pictured above. Interact with the nest to grab the feather.

Sundial of Ages location

The Sundial of Ages is near a bandit camp on a table surrounded by spears. Interact with it to grab the sundial.

Cup of Commons location

The Cup of Commons is actually locked behind a spin-off side quest. If you approach the bandit camp above, the side quest will begin. Following the quest and opening the chest at the end will reward you with the cup.

Helm of Warding location

The Helm of Warding is in a house near Wang. Follow the Seelie into the house and use the fire barrel to light the nearby hay ablaze to reveal the helm.

Warrior’s Spear location

The Warrior’s Spear is on top of a cliff, just sticking out by some sugar flowers.
