Oyo State Governorship Election Results 2023: Live Updates from INEC

August 06, 2024
post

The 2023 governorship election was successfully conducted in Oyo state on Saturday, March 18. The key contenders are: Seyi Makinde (PDP), Teslim Folarin (APC), and Bayo Adelabu (Accord Party).

The official collation of the results from 33 Local Government Areas (LGAs) is expected to commence Sunday, March 19, at the Independent National Electoral Commission (INEC)'s state collation centre in Ibadan, the state capital.

Stay tuned for live updates of the results from the 33 LGAs.
