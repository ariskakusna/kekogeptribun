&quot;University Blue&quot; Air Jordan 11 Lows Are Making A Return Next Year

August 11, 2024
post

The Carolina Blue "Columbia" Air Jordan 11 Low will be making a return in 2017 for the first time since the sneaker originally launched in 2001. 

According to AJordanxi.com, the iconic colorway will be just one of a handful of low-top 11s that are returning next year. Early reports also suggest that the color will be changed from "Columbia Blue" to "University Blue" and the shoes will likely have a blueish tint to their sole, unlike the OG pair which opted for a clear bottom.

In addition to this returning classic, Jordan Brand will also be continuing their "Barons" line with a "black/silver/white" Air Jordan 11 Low, as well as two women's releases in "blue moon/white" and "white/pure platinum." 

The men's sizes of the Air Jordan 11 Low will reportedly retail for $175 next year, but other release details regarding the "University Blue" 11s have not yet been announced- though a May release is rumored.
