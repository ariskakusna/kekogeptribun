Kristin Davis hasn't given up on SATC3 yet

August 14, 2024
post

Kim Cattrall may have repeatedly refused to appear in SATC3, but Kristin Davis remains optimistic that the project could still happen.

The actress, who played Samantha Jones in the show and its two films, said earlier this week that the cast had never been friends. She was recently forced to debunk reports that the only reason the third movie was not going ahead was because of her alleged diva demands.

Sarah Jessica Parker, consequently, said she was "disappointed" that a third film would not be made despite its "beautiful, funny, heartbreaking, joyful, very relatable script and story".

However, despite all the arguing, Davis, much like her on-screen character Charlotte York, believes a happy ending is still possible.

“I’m just grateful. I know my co-stars are grateful as well that we have the fan support, that we have each other, that we love each other,” she told E Online. “Maybe we’ll figure out something to do, I don’t know, it would be great."

When news broke that a SATC3 would not be happening, Davis shared her disappointment on Instagram.

"It is deeply frustrating not to able to share that chapter (beautifully written by MPK) with all of you," she wrote. "So we will just have our memories, but please know that all of the love and support for us through the years is felt by us and we are so grateful for all of you!"
