Emily Ratajkowski Put a Sporty-Chic Twist on the Pantless Trend

August 19, 2024
post

2023 was the year of no pants. Whether heading to the grocery store or the Met Gala, celebrities (Kendall Jenner, Hailey Bieber, Rihanna) left their pants at home with reckless abandon. And Emily Ratajkowski, for one, is determined to give the trend legs longterm. 

On Thursday, the model made an appearance in the front row at Puma's fall 2024 runway show in New York City while giving the pantless look a sporty twist. For the occasion, EmRata wore a long but fitted black track jacket that hit below her butt and featured a two-way zipper. In one direction, it zipped down past her bust, while in the other, it inched upward towards her midsection, revealing a teeny-tiny pair of matching black micro shorts underneath that gave the illusion that she had no pants on. 

Emily accessorized her all-black athleisure with a pair of bright red Puma sneakers and a black shoulder bag. Her brunette hair was worn down in loose waves with a middle part and her curtain bangs pushed off to the sides, while the rest of her glam consisted of dewy skin and flushed cheeks — no workout required. 

The night before, EmRata stepped out in another red-and-black outfit — this time, in reverse. For the evening outing, the My Body author paired a crimson minidress with black strappy stilettos and carried a cherry-red clutch. A sleek blowout, glossy lips, and a bronzy glow rounded out her look.
